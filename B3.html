The Three Jewels: Ratna-Trayi

The path to liberation (Moksh) consists of a trinity of jewels that must be cultivated together.

"Samyag-darshan-jnän-chäriträni Mokshamärgah"

– Tattvärtha Sutra

Samyag Darshan

(Right Perception)

Seeing the true nature of reality without bias. It is the foundation.

Samyag Jnän

(Right Knowledge)

The true knowledge of reality, the soul, and karma that compels proper action.

Samyak Chäritra

(Right Conduct)

Living according to the principles of non-violence, self-control, and non-attachment.

These three jewels are interdependent. Like three strands of a rope, they are strong together but weak alone.

The Soul (Ätmä)

The soul is the living substance within every being, distinguished by its quality of consciousness (Chetanä). We can't see it with our senses, like air or electricity, but we realize its existence through its operations.

8 Attributes of a Pure Soul

Anant-jnän (Infinite Knowledge)
Anant-darshan (Infinite Perception)
Avyäbädha-sukh (Eternal Happiness)
Anant-chäritra (Perfect Conduct)
Akshay-sthiti (Immortality)
Arupitva (Formlessness)
Aguru-laghutva (Equalness)
Anant-virya (Infinite Energy)

Pure Soul vs. Worldly Soul

✨ Pure Soul

Free, blissful, no karma, infinite qualities.

☁️ Worldly Soul

Ignorant, bound by karma, limited qualities, seeks pleasure in materialism.

The Spiritual Path (Gunasthän)

The journey of the soul from complete wrong belief to total liberation is a step-by-step process through 14 spiritual stages, or Gunasthän. Here are the key milestones.

Stage 1: Mithyätva

Complete wrong belief. Starting point for all worldly souls.

Stage 4: Samyaktva

Attainment of Right Faith. Faith Deluding Karma is destroyed.

Stages 5-7: Right Conduct Begins

Adopts vows, becomes a lay follower (5), then an ascetic (6-7).

Stage 12: Vitaräg

Passionless state. All Conduct Deluding Karma is destroyed.

Stage 13: Sayogi Kevali

Attainment of Keval-jnän (Omniscience). All 4 Ghāti Karmas are destroyed.

Stage 14 & Moksh

Ayogi Kevali stage (freezes activity), then sheds all 4 Aghāti Karmas at Nirvāna to attain total liberation.
